Link's Ingredients Quest

The task of gathering the ingredients sure wasn't easy, but throughout his journey, Link had a lot of fun, and he wants to share his experience with you.

Dubious Food

For the dubious food ingredients link had to fish, hunt, and mow the grass.

  • Here's a photo of Link finding a fish on the shore. He was very lucky.

  • Link went exploring with his bike, hoping to find a bokoblin, and he did!

  • Link's hard quest didn't end there, because he still had to find two crickets by cutting the grass.

Rock-Hard Food

For the rock-hard food ingredients, he had to go to Death Mountain, and attempt to obtain the ingredients without getting cooked in the process.

  • As you might've seen in the Dubious Food recipe, and above, Link obtained two bokoblin horns from the kind bokoblin he met.

  • Here you can see Link getting chuchu jelly by defeating some fire chuchu. He burned his kneecaps a bit in the process.

  • After almost being cooked alive, Link decided that it was time for some mining, and obtain a beautiful ruby.

Monster Cake

After all that running, hunting, and burning, Link decided that it was time to make things easier and buy the ingredients instead of fighting for them.

I'm joking. After running around throughout all Hyrule, he found out that those items can't be found unless you buy them, and ran straight to his favorite store where he actually found almost everything he needed.

  • Here you can see Link in his favorite convenience store, where he found 3 of the 4 ingredients he needed.

  • When the night came, Link went a payed a visit to his old friend Kilton and bought a bottle of totally legal monster extract. He know his way around Hyrule.
