Link: The Hero of Hyrule

Some know him as the hero of time, hero of the seas, or hero of the twilight. But regardless of how people call him, he is a hero.

A hero who likes to eat, a lot. Link is the reincarnation of the hero destined to defeat Ganondorf, the reincarnation of demise alongside the Princess Zelda, the reincarnation of the Goddess Hylia.

Regardless of how many times Link reincarnates, his nature will never change. He is a noble fella that has so much to show, and yet so little to tell.

A heart-breaker, a great friend and an excellent chef.
