Who is this Link?

Still want to know more about Link? Well, he is the Hero of Hyrule chosen by the sword that seals the darkness...

Throughout time, he's been called the hero of sky, time, twilight, etc. Regardless of how they call him, or how many times he reincarnates, he is a hero.

And a simple boy. He might be courageous and fearless when it comes to enemies, but he also has his worries. With so much at stake, and so many eyes upon him,

he feels it necessary to stay strong and to silently bear any burden. That is the reason why he is so quiet and makes it difficult for him to express his thoughts and feelings.

Everyone has struggles that go unseen by the world. We are just so absorbed with our own problems, that sometimes we fail to see them.

In The Legend Of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Link loses his memories and awakens without that heavy load of expectations and pressure from others. That's why we can see a more open and expressive Link.

I think, that if we were to forget all of our problems, and worries we would be like Link. A free and happy spirit.

Sadly, we can't just simply forget. It takes a lot of time, effort and help, but we can also defeat our demons. I know it can be hard sometimes, but I promise you this.

At the end, everything will be okay, and if it's not okay, we are still not at the end. So keep moving on, at yout on pace.

If you wish to try any of the Zelda games, I recommend you to try Breath of the Wild. It's my favorite game of all time, and in my opinion a good introduction to the franchise.
