Monster Cake

One of my favorite dishes in Breath of the Wild. It is said that once you get a taste of this cake you will never forget it sweetness.

And they also say that it's a dangerous dish that will make your head fuzzy and may even motivate you to scheme some mischevious plans.

Ingredients

  • Tabantha wheat
  • Cane sugar
  • Goat butter
  • And one bottle of monster extract

You can find some of these items in the wild, but items like the monster extract and goat butter, have to be bought in a store. You can find the first three items at your favortie local (and only one) Rito Village store.

The last item is a bit tricky to find. You will have to find a guy named Kilton that usually stays at the outskirts of any town. After you find Kilton, you can then illegally legally buy the monster extract from him.

Instructions

  1. Get half sheaf of tabantha wheat and throw it into the pot.
  2. Once your tabantha wheat gets a soaky texture, throw in a room temperature stick of goat butter.
  3. Stir the pot with a stick of cane sugar until you get a nice thick dough, and discard the stick of cane sugar.
  4. Grab a bottle of monster extract and carefully, add two drops of the extract. If we add more than two drops the cake will end up being too sweet!
  5. Cover the pot with a lid, and bake a 666°F degrees for about 666 seconds and serve.
