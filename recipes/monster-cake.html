Monster Cake

One of my favorite dishes in Breath of the Wild. It is said that once you get a taste of this cake you will never forget it sweetness.

And they also say that it's a dangerous dish that will make your head fuzzy and may even motivate you to scheme some mischevious plans.

Ingredients

  • Tabantha wheat
  • Cane sugar
  • Goat butter
  • And one bottle of monster extract

You can find some of these items in the wild, but items like the monster extract and goat butter, have to be bought in a store.

You can find the first three items at your favortie local (and only one) Rito Village store.

The last item is a bit tricky to find. You will have to find a guy named Kilton that usually stays at the outskirts of any town.

After you find Kilton, you can then illegally legally buy the monster extract from him.
