Rock-Hard Food

In my opinion this is indubitably one of the most underrated dishes in Breath of the Wild.

The roch-hard food dish. A simple, but also very filling and reliable dish for when you are between a rock and a hard place.

While eating rocks might not be a good idea for regular people, for Link is no trouble at all, and according to him, is very nutritious.

Ingredients

  • A cruelty free harvested Bokoblin Horn.
  • Two warm Red Chuchu Jelly.
  • A freshly harvested Ruby.
