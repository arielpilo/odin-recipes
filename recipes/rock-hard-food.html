Rock-Hard Food

In my opinion this is indubitably one of the most underrated dishes in Breath of the Wild.

The roch-hard food dish. A simple, but also very filling and reliable dish for when you are between a rock and a hard place.

While eating rocks might not be a good idea for regular people, for Link is no trouble at all, and according to him, is very nutritious.

Ingredients

  • A cruelty free harvested Bokoblin Horn.
  • Two warm Red Chuchu Jelly.
  • A freshly harvested Ruby.

After gathering all these items, all we have to do is find a hot pot so we can make this bad boy.

Instructions

  1. Take your Halal bokoblin horn and throw it into your cooking pot.
  2. Carefully add the two previosly warmed chuchu jellies, and add them to the pot. Careful while handling chuchu jelly since they are knonw to be extremely volatile.
  3. Grab your freshly harvested ruby, give it a nice polish and throw that bad boy into that pot.
  4. Wait until you can smell the volcanic fumes and remove from the heat inmediately. We don't want to overcook it, but we also want it to have a nice crispiness for our dish.
  5. Serve and enjoy your perfectly cooked roock-hard dish. Though be prepared. It's not easy to chew, or digest...
