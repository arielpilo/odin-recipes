Dubious Food

Although hard to look at or even smell, this is a great source of energy.

Made from high quality ingredients found in the wild, and of course, love.

It might not look edible, but eating it won't hurt... probably.

  • And one bokoblin horn

    • I found this one by bullying slaying a bokoblin I found while riding my bike.

And that's it!
