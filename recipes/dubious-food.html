Dubious Food

Although hard to look at or even smell, this is a great source of energy.

Made from high quality ingredients found in the wild, and of course, love.

It might not look edible, but eating it won't hurt... probably.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite fish. Link used one he found at the shore.

  • One bokoblin horn. Link found this one by bullying slaying a bokoblin he found while riding his bike.

  • (Optional) Two crickets. These can be found by cutting grass. Link found these under a rock.

Do you want to take a look at how Link managed to gather these fine ingredients?

Recipe instructions:

  1. Grab the most fresh fish you can find and throw it in the cooking pot.
  2. Stir the pot with a bokoblin horn until the fish starts to boil and then add the horn to the pot. This will add the crispiness to the dish.
  3. Sprinkle two crickets on top of the pot. This is optional, but this will give our dish that home made aroma that'll make you forget about the taste.
  4. Wait until the food gets a censored look and texture and serve!
